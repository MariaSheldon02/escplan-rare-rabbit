Context

What Is This All About?

The Brand

Rare Rabbit is a premium Indian menswear brand under The House of Rare. Its trolley line is manufactured and distributed by Escape Plan (Enchante Brands Pvt. Ltd., HSR Layout, Bengaluru) — a travel accessories platform co-founded by Abhinav Pathak and Abhinav Zutshi, launched May 2025.

The Platform

Escape Plan raised USD 5M seed in July 2025 and USD 25M Series A in January 2026 (Jungle Ventures, Fireside Ventures, IndiGo Ventures). Currently reports ₹300Cr+ ARR. Two trolley lines — Gallardo and Tony — sold exclusively online.

The Market

India's luggage market was valued at USD 4.0–4.26 billion in 2025, growing at 6.72% CAGR through 2034 (IMARC Group). Offline retail accounts for approximately 85% of total luggage sales by value — a channel Rare Rabbit does not yet operate in.

Why This Research

To identify the minimum product, distribution, and service thresholds required to reduce customer hesitation and churn — translating competitive findings into prioritised strategies that improve CLV and reduce pre-purchase drop-off.

Brand Analysis

Rare Rabbit — Strengths & Areas for Development

✅
Strengths
👑
Fashion Brand Credibility
The House of Rare reported ₹637 crore in revenue for FY24. Rare Rabbit's trust with urban professionals aged 25–40 extends naturally into luggage — an advantage no pure-play luggage brand can easily replicate.
🛡️
10-Year Warranty (Escape Plan / Amazon.in)
Exceeds Mokobara's 6-year (Iconic), Safari's 5-year, and American Tourister India's 3-year. A strong credential — subject to the channel inconsistency noted in weaknesses.
🔒
TSA Lock + PC Shell Across All SKUs
Removable toiletry module — noted positively in verified purchase reviews — is not a standard offering in competing brands at this price range.
✈️
IndiGo Ventures Strategic Investor
Confirmed in GoIndigo.in press release dated 22 January 2026. Opens airport retail and in-flight catalogue pathways that no other new-entrant luggage brand in India currently has.
⚠️
Areas for Development
📉
Limited SKU Range (2 Lines)
Mokobara offers 30+ SKUs; American Tourister 50+ models. A buyer seeking a soft-sided bag, laptop trolley, or sub-₹4,000 entry option finds nothing in Rare Rabbit's range today.
🏬
Online-Only Distribution
85% of India's luggage sales value flows through offline channels. VIP Industries has 8,000+ retail outlets; Safari covers pan-India including tier II/III. Rare Rabbit is absent from all physical retail as of May 2026.
🔧
No Repair / Service Infrastructure
Warranty support via email and phone only. Zero authorised repair centres. VIP operates 300+; American Tourister uses Samsonite India's authorised service network.
⚖️
Cabin Weight Not Published
Gallardo medium is 3.5 kg. Cabin weight unpublished on any channel. Mokobara lists 3.12–3.27 kg explicitly. With IndiGo's 7 kg domestic cabin limit, buyers need this at point of purchase.
📋
Hardware Specs Unnamed
No product page names shell grade, wheel manufacturer, or zipper brand. Mokobara markets German Makrolon, Japanese Hinomoto spinners, and YKK zippers. Side-by-side comparisons favour Mokobara.
🔀
Warranty Channel Inconsistency
Products carry 10-year warranty on myescplan.com and Amazon.in, but only 5-year on thehouseofrare.com. Thorough buyers lose confidence in the headline claim.

Roadmap to Market Leadership 2026–2027

Priority Recommendations

9 Prioritised Actions

🔴 Critical
Publish Shell Grade, Wheel Manufacturer & Zipper Brand on All Product Pages
Buyers comparing specs will find Mokobara's Makrolon, Hinomoto, and YKK claims more reassuring than unnamed equivalents. Publishing equivalent detail levels the comparison in side-by-side research.
🔴 Critical
Publish Cabin Weight on All Product Listings
With IndiGo's 7 kg domestic cabin limit, buyers need this before purchase. Mokobara lists 3.12–3.27 kg on product pages. Rare Rabbit currently lists no figure for any SKU.
🔴 Critical
Expand Trolley Range — Minimum 6 SKUs
Include a sub-₹5,000 cabin, a laptop trolley, and a large standalone check-in. Two lines limit addressable demand significantly. Every format gap redirects a potential buyer to a competitor.
🟠 High
Establish Authorised Service Touchpoints in Key Metro Cities
Partnering with existing luggage repair centres is a practical near-term step toward the service credibility that VIP (300+ centres) and American Tourister hold. Email-only support is insufficient for a premium-priced product.
🟠 High
Engineer and Communicate a Verified Sub-2.8 kg Cabin Bag
A documented lightweight claim would directly appeal to domestic air travellers and differentiate Rare Rabbit from Mokobara's 3.23–3.27 kg range — a meaningful technical gap to exploit.
🟠 High
Publish Durability Content — Drop Tests, Wheel Stress Tests, Multi-Trip Reviews
Buyers consistently note the absence of long-trip evidence. Third-party tested content addresses this gap more credibly than brand copy alone. Every current positive review references first-time use only.
🟡 Medium
Activate the IndiGo Ventures Relationship for Airport Retail Access
This distribution pathway is unique to Rare Rabbit among new-entrant luggage brands and is time-sensitive. No other new entrant has this confirmed investor relationship with a major domestic airline.
🟡 Medium
Introduce Trolley Display & Cross-Sell at Rare Rabbit Apparel Stores
The existing network of 200+ apparel stores provides a warm, high-intent audience at low incremental acquisition cost. This also addresses the 85% offline sales gap without requiring a full retail build-out.

Marketing Strategy

Creating Demand & Improving Positioning

CONTENT STRATEGY

Build Long-Trip Evidence

Commission 3–5 content creators to document multi-city trips with Gallardo / Tony. Buyers see first-use reviews only — long-trip durability content directly addresses the #1 hesitation point.

SPEC MARKETING

Name Your Hardware

Mokobara wins spec comparisons by naming Makrolon, Hinomoto, and YKK. Publishing equivalent (or superior) specs levels the playing field in the research phase where buyers compare multiple tabs.

CHANNEL ACTIVATION

IndiGo Airport Retail

Leverage IndiGo Ventures' strategic relationship to place Gallardo at departure gate retail. Captures high-intent buyers at the exact moment of travel — no other new-entrant has this access.

CROSS-SELL

Apparel Store Integration

Display trolleys in 200+ Rare Rabbit apparel stores. Customers buying a ₹5,000 shirt are already pre-qualified for a ₹8,000 trolley. No new customer acquisition cost.

TRUST BUILDING

3rd-Party Durability Testing

Video drop tests, wheel stress tests, and multi-trip user studies published openly. Builds the "track record" in luggage that Mokobara currently has and Rare Rabbit lacks.

PRODUCT DIFFERENTIATION

Sub-2.8kg Campaign

Engineer, certify, and market a verified sub-2.8 kg cabin bag. With IndiGo's 7 kg cabin limit, a 2.7 kg trolley gives 4.3 kg of actual packing weight — a concrete, measurable benefit Mokobara cannot match.
